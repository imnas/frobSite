What is FROB?

FROB is a Byteball digital asset token designed to disrupt the PvP gaming market. FROB uses smart contracts and oracles to provide fair, untraceable transactions for casual gamers. It can also be exchanged for Bitcoin using a decentralised peer-to-peer platform. FROB is used to compete in PvP (player versus player) games created by FROB developers and community members. FROB will use oracles and smart contracts to provide proof of fair play in each PvP game. FROB offers untraceable peer-to-peer transactions.
